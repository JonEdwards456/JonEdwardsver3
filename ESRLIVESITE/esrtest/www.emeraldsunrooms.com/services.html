Services

  • ESR is a locally owned company that has been doing business in central Texas since 1986. By being local we allow stable job growth within the community, and all of the money goes back into the communities for future prosperity.
  • ESR is an environmentally conscious builder by choosing green building products for each new project. Building Green not only helps the environment, but provides clean air quality within the home, and by doing so, reduces energy consumption by as much as 30%.

These methods include:

  • Geo-thermal heating and cooling
  • programmable thermostats
  • Rigid foam insulation in rafter cavities as well as wall cavities
  • On demand hot water and circulating pumps
  • Low glass that exceeds city energy rated requirements
  • Low or even no VOC interior paints
