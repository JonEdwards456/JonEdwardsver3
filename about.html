About Jon Edwards

About

Welcome to my personal website for my art! My name is Jon Edwards, b. 1976 in Austin, TX. I currently am a freelance graphic designer, entering contests and finding commissions when I can. (I have a day job as well as a waiter.) I received my BFA from UT Austin in 2001, and have been making art ever since! Lately I have been working more on Web Development. Austin is a great community for the arts, and I often enjoy the many museums and art festivals here. View my resume, see the link on my website footer! Please be sure to take a look at my online portfolio in the menu link above! If you have any questions, comments, feedback, or just want to say hello, visit the contact link above.
