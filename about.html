About

Welcome to my personal website for my art! My name is Jon Edwards, b. 1976 in Austin, TX. I currently am continuing to develop my skills as a web developer, graphic designer, and visual artist. (I have a day job as a waiter as well.) I received my BFA from UT Austin in 2001, and have been making art ever since! Lately I have been working more on Web Development. Austin is a great community for the arts, and I often enjoy the many museums and art festivals here. Please explore this small website to see my skillset and recent projects.
